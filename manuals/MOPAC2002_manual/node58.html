Linear Scaling

A new method for solving the SCF equations is implemented. In this method, the time required for calculating the SCF increases linearly with the size of the system. The memory demand is also considerably reduced (for the larger systems run, by 97 to 98 percent of what would be needed conventionally). This allows systems of many thousands of atoms to be calculated rapidly.
