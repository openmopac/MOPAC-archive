Vibrations

The normal modes of vibration of a stationary system can be calculated. For ground states, this consists of the 3N-6 or 3N-5 non-zero modes, while for simple transition states, the 3N-7 real modes and 1 imaginary mode are reported. If desired, the force constants for the system can be printed.
