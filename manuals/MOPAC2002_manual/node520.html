Lewis Structure--Main Sequence.

1.
The connectivity is calculated. This determines which atom is bonded to which atom.
2.
All atoms that have explicit charges are identified, and the charges assigned.
3.
The s framework is determined.
4.
Most of the lone pairs are identified.
5.
Open-ended (non-aromatic) p-bonds are identified.
6.
Aromatic p-bonds are identified.
7.
All cations, anions, and any remaining lone pairs are identified.
