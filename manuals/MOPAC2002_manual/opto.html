OPT-O

Turn on all optimization flags for oxygen atoms.  This keyword is useful for partial geometry optimizations: first turn all optimization flags off, then use  OPT-O to selectively turn on the optimization flags for oxygen atoms.  See also OPT-C, OPT-H, OPT-N, and OPT-S.
