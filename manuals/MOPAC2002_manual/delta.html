DELTA

In a CROSS calculation, there are two termination criteria, gradient norm and energy difference, both of which must be satisfied before the calculation will normally end. The energy difference criterion between the two states involved has a default value of 0.04 kcal/mol; this can be changed using DELTA=n.nn The gradient criterion is 0.01; this can be changed by use of GNORM=n.nn .
