Main electronic flow

The main conventional electronic sequence in MOPAC is given in the Figure.

  

Figure: Diagram of Main Conventional Electronic Sequence in MOPAC

The LMO method is similar to the conventional method. The main changes are:

  • HQRII and DIAG are replaced by DIAGG (the annihilation operation).
  • MECI, MECIP, etc, and INTERP are not available.
  • Extra subroutines, used in creating the initial LMOs, are present.
