MINDO/3

The default Hamiltonian within MOPAC is MNDO, with the alternatives of MNDO-d, PM5, PM3, AM1 and MINDO/3. To use the MINDO/3 Hamiltonian [2] the keyword MINDO/3 should be used. Acceptable alternatives to the keyword MINDO/3 are MINDO and MINDO3.
