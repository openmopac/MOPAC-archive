MANUAL

This word was used in the writing and de-bugging of DENSITY, but now is only intended for testing and demonstration purposes.

When MANUAL is specified, either DMAT or M.O. must be specified on the same line as MANUAL.

If MANUAL is specified, the following data must be supplied on the next few lines:

1. Number of atoms, orbitals, and electrons (NATOMS, NORBS, NELECS). One line, free format, integer.
 
2. Cartesian coordinates for each atom, one atom per line, free format, real, in order x, y, z. There will be NATOMS of these.
 
3. Labels for all atoms. For example, all carbon atoms might be called "1", all hydrogen atoms "2", etc. Free format, integer. There will be NATOMS of these numbers. The numbers must be 1 to NUNI, NUNI being the number of unique atom-types in the molecule.
