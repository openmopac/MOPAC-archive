Programs recommended for use with MOPAC

MOPAC is the core program of a series of programs for the theoretical study of chemical phenomena. This version is the latest in an on-going development, and efforts are being made to continue its further evolution. In order to make using MOPAC easier, three other programs have also been written. Users of MOPAC are recommended to use all the programs. Efforts will be made to continue the development of these programs.

These programs are

  • BZ
  • MAKPOL
