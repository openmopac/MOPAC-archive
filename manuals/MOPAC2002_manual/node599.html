U = Eeq + [Evib + Erot + Etra]

or

U = Eeq + [(Evib + Evib (T))+ Erot + Etra]

Enthalpy H for one mole of gas is defined as

H = U + pV  

Assumption of an ideal gas (i.e., pV = RT) leads to

H = U + pV = U + RT 

Thus, Gibbs free energy G can be calculated as:

G = H - T S(T)
