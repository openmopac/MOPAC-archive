Normal Coordinate Calculation

Normal coordinate calculations involve determining the direction of motion of the pure (quantized) vibrations of a system, and their associated energies of vibration.  For the general case of a non-linear molecule there are 3N-6 non-trivial modes; for a linear molecule, there are 3N-5, for a linear polymer, there are 3N-4, and for a solid, there are 3N-3 normal modes.,
