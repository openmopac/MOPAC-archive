Introduction

Most of the theory used in MOPAC is in the literature, so that in principle one could read and understand the algorithm. However, it is convenient to have the theory gathered together in one document. The theory given here is intended for users who (a) want to modify MOPAC, or (b) want to understand how MOPAC does what it does. All derivations can be followed with a little patience, and, as far as possible, "It can be shown that" jumps in logic have been avoided.
