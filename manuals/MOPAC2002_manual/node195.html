HYPERFINE

The quantity:

(1/3)(Psa - Psb +2(ysa)2)

is printed for all atoms in a radical when HYPERFINE is specified for a UHF system. The ysa refers to the highest occupied alpha-spin molecular orbital. This quantity is of use in predicting hyperfine coupling coefficients.
