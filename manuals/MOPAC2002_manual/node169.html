EF

The Eigenvector Following routine [7] is the default geometry optimization method. Keyword EF does nothing, but is provided to allow explicit definition of the optimizer. The alternative geometry optimizer is the BFGS [19,20,21,22] method. See also DDMAX, DDMIN, DMAX, GNMIN, HESS, IUPD, LET, MODE=, NONR, OMIN, PRNT=, RECALC, RMAX, RMIN, RSCAL.

The current EF, while very reliable, is sometimes slow. To over-ride some safety checks, specify LET. This will sometimes make the job run faster.
