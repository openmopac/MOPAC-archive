MINDO/3 Two electron two center integrals

In marked contrast to the other methods, MINDO/3 Coulomb and exchange integrals are given by the simple Dewar-Sabelli [56,57] - Klopman [58] approximation. The integral is a function of the atom types and the interatomic distance only, and is of form
