BFGS

The default geometry optimizer, EF, is unsuitable for large (over about 500-1,000 atoms) systems. For large systems, the suggested optimizer is the Broyden-Fletcher-Goldfarb-Shanno [19,20,21,22] procedure. By specifying BFGS, this procedure will be used instead of EF. For very large systems, the LBFGS optimizer is best.
