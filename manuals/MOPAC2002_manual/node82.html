Thermodynamics

Various thermodynamic quantities (Partition function, enthalpy, heat capacity, and entropy) can be calculated for any temperature, or range of temperatures. These quantities can be decomposed into vibrational, rotational, internal, and translational contributions. The effect of changing temperature on the DHf can be monitored.
