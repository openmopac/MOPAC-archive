Faults and Errors in PM5

  1. The barrier to rotation in formamide is too low. In part, this can be corrected by the use of the MMOK option, see Correction to the Peptide Linkage.
  2. The LUMO of methane is of symmetry a1 instead of the correct symmetry of t2.
  3. The hydrogen bond length is too long (2.68 A instead of the observed ~2.0 A)
PM5 is still very new, and as faults are discovered, they will be added to this list.
