COSMO Keywords

EPS=n.n Defines the dielectric constant of the solvent. This keyword triggers the whole COSMO.
NSPA=nn Controls the number of segments, default = 42.
DISEX=n.n Controls the radius, up to which the segment-segment interactions are evaluated on the basis of the basic grid points. Default = 2.0. For accurate calculations or very high dielectric energies*, (e.g. ions) larger values may be preferable. The calculation time may increase as DISEX2 (until all interactions are calculated accurately).
RSOLV=n.n Effective VDW radius of the solvent molecule. Default = 1.0Å.

Footnotes

* : The dielectric energy is the energy of stabilization arising from the interaction of the charges in the solute with the induced charges on the solvent accessible surface plus the electrostatic energy due to the charges on the SAS interacting with each other.
