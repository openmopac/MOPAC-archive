Structure of this Manual

 This manual gives a full description of the data, working, and structure of the MOPAC program. Although the manual can be read like a book - going from one "page" to another - most of the time, users will want to read about a specific topic. The suggested ways of using the manual as a follows:

  • To read it like a book: click on the icon or the hypertext link after the word "Next:" at the top of the page to go to the next page. To go to the previous page click on the icon or the hypertext link after the word "Previous:".
  • To go to a specific section, first go to the home page (click on the icon at the top of a page), then use the hyperlinks on that page to locate the section of interest.
  • If information on a specific topic, for example, spin states, is wanted, first go to the home page, then start the left-most search engine, the one that looks like this:

Search
+ Tips

  • If the information is likely to be in only one or at most only a very few pages, first go to the home page, then start the search engine second from the left, the one that looks like this:

Search
highlight

This is particularly useful when searching for specific molecules.
  • A normal index is available, to use it click on the icon and then click on the blue ball adjacent to the entry of interest.
  • If the information needed is likely to be in a Figure or Table, most of them are in the page here.

The pages are designed to be printed. Most are quite small, but some, particularly the tables of Heats of Formation and Geometries, are very long. Do not print these unless there is a definite need to do so.

Within the tables in the Accuracy section, all molecule names are hyperlinks to the data sets. These data sets should be viewed with, e.g. CHIME, available from http://www.mdlchime.com/chime/main.asp. To check if your browser already has CHIME running, look in the following space:

If you see a picture of a neopentane molecule, then CHIME is already installed. If nothing, or anything else, appears then CHIME is not installed.
