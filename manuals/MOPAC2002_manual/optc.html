OPT-C

Turn on all optimization flags for carbon atoms.  This keyword is useful for partial geometry optimizations: first turn all optimization flags off, then use  OPT-C to selectively turn on the optimization flags for carbon atoms.  See also OPT-H, OPT-N, OPT-O, and OPT-S.
