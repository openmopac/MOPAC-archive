Bond Orders

Three quantities can be derived [15] from the density matrix for use in discussing bonding. These are: atomic bond index, anisotropy, and bond order.

The density matrix, P, can be decomposed into sub-matrices representing atoms or interactions between atoms. The three quantities just mentioned can then be defined in terms of these sub-matrices.
