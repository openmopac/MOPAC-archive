Lewis Structures

The Lewis structures for most systems can be printed. A limitation is that systems involving atoms with more than four bonds cannot be handled, although in practice, this can partly be circumvented by breaking bonds. Systems as complicated as fullerene, TCNQ, SF6, cystein zwitterion, and benzene, can be described.
