COSCAV/COSCAN (COSmo CAVity)

This routine constructs the SAS and calculates and inverts the surface charge interaction matrix A.

Then the new transformation matrix is built from the local coordinate system defined by the nearest neighbors. The transformation to a local coordinate system guarantees a higher stability of the segmentation during geometry optimization. Then the basic grid of direction vectors is transformed according to the new transformation matrix.
