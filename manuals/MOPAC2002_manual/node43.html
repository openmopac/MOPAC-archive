Frequency dependent Non-Linear Optics

In addition to the polarizability, the following non-linear optical properties can be calculated: first and second order properties (b and g), Electric Field Second Harmonic Generation (EFISH), optical rectification, electrooptic Pockel's Effect, second and third harmonic generation. Where appropriate, averages are printed.
