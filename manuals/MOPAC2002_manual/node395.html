BONDS

Not to be used in conjunction with HOMO, LUMO, PSI=n, or OCCUPANCY. BONDS calculates the average atomic orbital occupancy of each atom, and subtracts that number from the density matrix. The result is a plot whose average value is zero, and which shows where the electrons have come from and gone to when the bonds are formed.
