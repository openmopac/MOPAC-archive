Level of Precision within MOPAC

Several users have criticized the tolerances within MOPAC. The point made is that significantly different results have been obtained when different starting conditions have been used, even when the same conformer should have resulted. Of course, different results must be expected--there will always be small differences--nonetheless any differences should be small, for example, heats of formation (DHf) differences should be less than about 0.1 kcal/mol. MOPAC has the flexibility to allow users to specify a much higher precision than the default when circumstances warrant it.
