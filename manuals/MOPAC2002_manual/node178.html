FIELD=(n.nn,m.mm,l.ll)

An external electric field of intensity n.nn volts/Ångstrom in the x-direction, m.mm volts/Ångstrom in the y-direction, and l.ll volts/Ångstrom in the z-direction is to be applied. An ion will be strongly affected by such a field. For example, Cl-1 at coordinates 1.00, 0.00, 0.00, would be stabilized by 1.0eV if a field were to be applied by specifying FIELD=(1.0,0.0,0.0).
