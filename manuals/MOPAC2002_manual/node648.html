Warnings

  • The solute geometry cannot be properly optimized in solution. Optimization flags at the different coordinates should be set to 0

  • The program has been carefully parametrized to work with neutral molecules. A reduction of the cavity for charged solutes may be necessary [94].

  • We cannot recommend the use of the program for solvents other than water, chloroform, or carbon tetrachloride at the present moment. A simple change in the dielectric constant cannot be enough to reproduce the characteristics of other solvents.
