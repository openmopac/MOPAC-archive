Giant Molecules

Using conventional matrix algebra, semiempirical methods are limited to about 600 atoms. This is a result of the N3 dependency of the time required for diagonalization and for construction of the density matrix. Using Localized Molecular Orbitals, the time-dependency can be lowered to, theoretically, N1, in practice, N1.4.
