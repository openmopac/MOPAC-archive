X-PRIORITY

In a DRC calculation, results will be printed whenever the calculated geometry changes by 0.05 Å. The geometry change is defined as the linear sum of the translation vectors of motion for all atoms in the system. Abbreviation, X-PRIO. See Dynamic and Intrinsic Reaction Coordinates for more details.
