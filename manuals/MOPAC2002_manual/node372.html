Lines in k-space

A line in k-space is specified by two numbers for a polymer, four numbers for a layer system, and six numbers for a solid. The number of points calculated for each line is proportional to the length of the line in k-space. For a line of length 1.0, for example G  to G, 300 points would be calculated.    The data needed to specify a band-structure 'walk' from G to X to W to L to Gis given in the Table, and for diamond, the band-structure for this 'walk' is given in the Figure.
