MAKPOL

MAKPOL, which comes with MOPAC, constructs the data-set for solids. Such data-sets can be made by hand, but are tedious to make, and prone to errors, in that the data must be entered in a specific way. As a result, users who intend to use the band-structure capability within the solid-state option, are encouraged to also acquire MAKPOL.
